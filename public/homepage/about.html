About Dunper AI

Dunper AI is building the AI receptionist for small businesses in Indonesia and Southeast Asia. We help local cafes, salons, dental clinics, and other shops handle the messages, questions, and bookings they're too busy to answer themselves.

Our mission

Make it possible for any small business to never miss a customer — at any hour.

Why now

Modern AI is finally good enough to answer in the customer's language, understand hand-written menus, and book appointments correctly. Customers expect instant replies on WhatsApp; we let small businesses meet that expectation without hiring more staff.
