What it does

Hire one AI. Cover every channel.

💬

Web chat + WhatsApp

Drops into your existing website. Replies on your business WhatsApp number. Same brain, both channels.

📅

Books real appointments

Real-time slot availability, your business hours, 24-hour notice rule. Confirmed events appear in your Google Calendar.

🌏

Bilingual out of the box

Customers chat in English or Bahasa Indonesia. The AI matches their language automatically.

🧠

Trained on your business

Upload your menu, price sheet, or policies as PDFs. Edit hours and rules in plain language. The AI grounds every reply in your real info.

📊

Owner dashboard

Live metrics, customer summaries, sentiment, conversion rate, top services, escalations — all in one place.

🤝

Human handoff

One tap and a customer can request a real person. You see them in your dashboard, reply via WhatsApp.

📎

Customers can send photos

"Here's a photo of my chipped tooth." The AI sees the image and responds. Useful for service intake.

🔒

Your data, your account

Your business connects its own Google account. Your data is yours. Per-business deployment, no shared tenants.

How it works

From signup to live in under an hour.

1

Tell Dunper about your business

Name, hours, services, prices, booking rules, tone of voice. Edit in a form, or just chat with the dashboard's AI assistant in plain language.

2

Connect Google + WhatsApp

One click connects your Google Calendar and a Sheet. WhatsApp Business runs through the official Meta Cloud API — works on your real number.

3

Embed and go live

Drop the chat snippet on your website. Hand out your WhatsApp number. The AI works 24/7, you read the highlights in the morning.
