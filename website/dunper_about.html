About Us

Building the future of
customer conversations

Dunper AI was founded on a simple idea: small businesses deserve the same caliber of customer service tools as the biggest brands. We're a team of builders who believe AI should feel like an employee — not a chatbot.

Our Mission

Give every business an AI
employee that actually shows up

The phone rings while you're with a client. A WhatsApp message arrives at 11pm. A booking inquiry comes through a website you barely have time to maintain. Every missed message is a missed customer.

Dunper AI is the AI receptionist that's always on duty. It answers questions in your customer's language, books appointments straight into your calendar, and only escalates to you when it really matters. So you can focus on the work itself — and we'll handle everything around it.
