About Dunper

The story behind Dunper

Dunper is the always-on AI receptionist for small businesses. We answer every call, every message, every booking — in any language, any hour.

01 · Background

Where we came from

Dunper started with a simple observation: small businesses lose customers every day because they can't answer fast enough. A salon misses a booking inquiry on Instagram while the owner is mid-haircut. A dentist returns a voicemail four hours late and the patient has already booked elsewhere. A restaurant in Jakarta gets WhatsApp messages in Bahasa, English, and Mandarin — with one person trying to answer them all.

We're a team of builders from Jakarta who grew up watching family, friends, and the businesses around us juggle this problem with sticky notes and shared phones. Dunper began as FrontDesk — an internal experiment to see if an AI receptionist could realistically run the front of a real business. Once it could, we rebranded and turned it into a product.

02 · Process

How Dunper was designed

We didn't start by designing a chatbot. We started by sitting in real businesses, watching how questions actually come in, and figuring out where AI could earn its keep — and where it shouldn't get involved.

STEP 01

Watch first, code later

Before writing a line of product code, we mapped every type of message that came through a dentist, a salon, and a small restaurant over two weeks. Patterns appeared fast — most questions were the same ones, over and over.

STEP 02

Build for the worst day

We designed Dunper to handle the chaos: simultaneous WhatsApp threads, broken English, urgent rescheduling requests, and the customer who just wants to know if you're still open. Calm days are easy — busy ones are the test.

STEP 03

Escalate, don't fake it

When Dunper doesn't know something, it says so and brings in a human. We'd rather route a question than hallucinate an answer. Trust compounds; pretending to be smarter than you are breaks it.

03 · What We Do

An AI that actually shows up

Dunper is an AI receptionist that answers questions, books appointments, and qualifies leads on behalf of small businesses — instantly, in 60+ languages, 24 hours a day. It lives inside your existing channels: WhatsApp, your website chat, even the phone line.

💬

Always-on conversations

Every message gets a response in seconds, no matter the time of day. Customers stop waiting; you stop losing them.

📅

Booking that just works

Appointments flow straight into your Google Calendar. Confirmations, reminders, and reschedules happen automatically.

🌍

Speaks your customers' language

60+ languages, including Bahasa, English, Mandarin, Spanish, and Tagalog. Dunper switches naturally mid-conversation.

04 · Why Dunper

Why teams choose us

There's no shortage of chatbots. Most are generic toys bolted onto a website. Dunper is built specifically for small service businesses, by people who care about the difference between something that demos well and something that runs your front desk.

⚡

Built for real businesses

Not a generic chatbot dropped onto a homepage. Tuned for salons, dentists, clinics, restaurants, and the SMEs that make neighborhoods work.

🔒

Your data stays yours

Each business connects its own Google account. We don't pool, share, or sell your customer data. Ever.

📐

Honest engineering

If Dunper doesn't know, it says so. No fake confidence, no hallucinated answers — just a clean handoff to a real person.

05 · Vision & Mission

Where we are headed

🎯

Our Vision

A world where every local business has the same caliber of customer service as the biggest brands — without needing to hire a single new person.

🚀

Our Mission

Give every small business an AI employee that actually shows up. Reliable, multilingual, and honest enough to admit when a human needs to step in.
